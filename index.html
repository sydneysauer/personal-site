About Me

Currently: Frontend web development intern at JB Hunt Transport & completing the #100daysofcode challenge.

I'm a computer science student with a Spanish minor, and I'm obsessed with learning, creating, and inventing. In the past, I've worked as a freelance writer and a math tutor (and a smoothie chef, for what it's worth). Now, I'm finally starting my first internship in computer science.
